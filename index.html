Akhbar Elyom Academy History

Founded : 1999

Founded in 1999, Akhbar Elyom Academy is a higher Education Institute, Member of AKHBAR ELYOM association, Under the Supervision of Ministry of Higher Education. The Academy awards certified certificates in the Department of Production Engineering and Printing Technology, Industrial Engineering Department, Computer Science and Information Technology Department, Journalism Department, (English) Administration Department, Electronics and Communications Engineering Department.
